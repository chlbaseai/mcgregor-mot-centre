Our Services

What we do best

We are first class mechanics with a well-equipped workshop, offering you the widest possible selection of services — all at highly competitive prices.

01
MOT Testing
VOSA approved 40+ point inspection. Same-day results with clear, honest reporting on every check.

The MOT tests important items on your vehicle to check they meet legal standards. The test does not constitute a full vehicle service and excludes evaluation of engine, clutch, and gearbox mechanical conditions.

Body & Structure
Checked for excessive corrosion or damage and sharp edges that could cause injury.
Towbars
Secureness and condition verified. 13-pin electrical socket functionality tested. Speedometer and engine mountings checked.
Fuel System
Leak detection. Pipe and hose security assessed. Fuel cap fastening verified.
Exhaust & Emissions
Security and completeness verified. Catalyst presence confirmed. Leak and noise assessment.
Brakes & ABS
Condition and operation checked. Anti-lock braking system and electronic stability control tested. Dashboard warning lights verified.
Seatbelts & Doors
Mandatory seatbelts present and functional. Driver seat adjustability verified. Door latching and mirror condition inspected.
Tyres & Wheels
Tread depth, size, type, and condition assessed. Registration plate condition and character formation inspected.
Lights & Steering
Headlamp aim checked. Wiper and washer functionality verified. Steering oil level and suspension condition tested.

03
Engine Diagnostics
State-of-the-art diagnostic equipment to quickly and accurately pinpoint problems.

Using specialised software, car diagnostic tools quickly and accurately point to problem areas in a car's engine or elsewhere, thanks to built-in processors, microchips and sensors.

Diagnostic tests can reveal problems within a car's engine, transmission, exhaust system, brakes, and other major components, as well as performance issues with the fuel injector, air flow and coolant, ignition coils, and throttle.

Many technicians recommend taking your car in for a diagnostic check at least once a year, which will uncover minor problems that can't be seen or heard and won't trigger the check engine light. You don't have to wait for a warning light — if you hear or feel something "off," we can use diagnostic testing to uncover the problem.

In addition to keeping your engine running smoothly, our fully qualified mechanics can perform mechanical repairs ranging from replacement exhaust systems to brakes, shock absorbers, timing belts and clutches. If it's in — or on — your car, we can fix it.

05
Tyres
Supply, fitting and free tyre checks. All brands and sizes at competitive prices.

UK law requires that your vehicle is fitted with the correct type and size of tyre for the vehicle type you are driving. The legal limit for minimum tread depth is 1.6 millimetres, across the central three-quarters of the tread around the complete circumference of the tyre.

For safety, manufacturers recommend replacing at 3 millimetres. A regular tyre check can help you avoid 3 penalty points and up to £2,500 in fines per worn tyre.

McGregor MOT Centre provides free tyre checks. Our trained technicians inspect general condition, verify tread depth compliance, and check pressure against manufacturer recommendations — with findings detailed in a written report.
